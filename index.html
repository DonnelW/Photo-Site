About me

At Charli Jayne Photography, I believe every person and every story is beautifully created with purpose. As a Christian photographer based in Murphy, North Carolina, my passion is capturing authentic moments that reflect love, joy, and the beauty of God's creation. Whether photographing families, couples, seniors, or special milestones, I strive to create a comfortable experience that allows genuine emotions to shine through. My hope is that every photograph becomes more than just an image it becomes a lasting reminder of the moments and people that matter most.
